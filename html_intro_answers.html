Zootopia

About the Movie

Zootopia is a 2016 animated film by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Set in a city where animals of all shapes and sizes coexist, the story follows a bunny cop and a con artist fox as they unravel a mystery that threatens the harmony of their society.

Main Characters

Judy Hopps

Judy is a determined and optimistic rabbit who becomes the first bunny police officer in Zootopia. She is brave and passionate about making a difference in her city.

Nick Wilde

Nick is a sly and witty fox who makes a living as a con artist. Despite his rough exterior, he proves to be a loyal and resourceful ally to Judy.

Chief Bogo

Chief Bogo is the buffalo in charge of the Zootopia Police Department. He is initially skeptical of Judy but comes to appreciate her talents and persistence.

Exploring Zootopia

The Plot

The story follows Judy and Nick as they team up to solve a case involving missing animals. Their journey uncovers a conspiracy that challenges societal norms and highlights the importance of unity and understanding.

The Setting

Zootopia is a vibrant metropolis divided into districts like Sahara Square, Tundratown, and the Rainforest District, each reflecting the diverse habitats of its inhabitants.
